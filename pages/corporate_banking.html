Best Bank in Corporate Banking

Typically, the big companies will only deal with banks that measure up to their needs and quality standards.

The panel of judges looks at the banks most preferred by the top companies listed at the Nairobi Stock Exchange based on market capitalization. The banks are then ranked on the basis of which is the most preferred and how they are rated by their corporate clients.
